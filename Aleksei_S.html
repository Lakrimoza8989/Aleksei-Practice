Sweet Recipe Baking time
Fiska v banke 150g butter, 120g brown sugar, 80g white sugar, 1 egg, 1 tsp vanilla, 220g flour, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp salt, 200g chocolate chips. Cream butter and sugars, add egg and vanilla, mix dry ingredients, combine, fold in chips. Bake at 180°C for 10–12 minutes.
